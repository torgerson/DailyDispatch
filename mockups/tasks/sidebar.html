⚠ Sidebar Design Decisions

  • Today view: Proposed as top-level nav. Would show today's tasks + today's feed entries in one view. Is this wanted, or is Feed + Tasks sufficient?
  • Notes list: Shown as a reverse-chron list in the sidebar. Could also be tag-filtered or search-first. This replaces the current "Recent" section panel approach.
  • Tasks nav item: Opens the Kanban/List tab. Count badge shows open (non-done) tasks. The current DailyDispatch tab bar would be replaced by this sidebar nav for primary navigation.
  • Calendar stays: Kept from the current app. Clicking a date filters the Feed to that day.
  • Tags stay: Compact cloud at the bottom. Could also move into the Feed view as a filter bar.
  • Search: Opens a search panel (existing Cmd+K behavior). Could be inline in sidebar or a full-tab view.
  • Settings/Gallery: Not shown here — would they be footer icons, or accessible via a menu? Currently they're tab-bar icons.
